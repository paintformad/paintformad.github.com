Company Overview
Make A Difference is a platform that empowers youth to become change leaders who make positive social impact and create self sustaining communities. On one hand, we work with Children at Risk in orphanages and street shelters, while at the same time, developing leadership talent among youth in India. Currently, India's fastest growing youth teacher network!
